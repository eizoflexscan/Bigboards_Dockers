General idea

Global outlier approaches apply a model to represent normal data points - Outliers are points that do not fit to that model. In the case of Multivariate data analysis , it means that, given a certain kind of statistical model, outliers are points that deviate from model predicted values.
Deviations from model predicted values are plotted in a control charts . Those charts derive from data a center line, an upper and a lower bound, highlighting as outliers all records out of those limits.






Assumptions

  • Normal data objects follow a (known) statistical model.
  • Outliers deviate strongly from this statistical model.

Model Limits

  • Basic model assumes only one normal generating mechanism , but there is usually more than one generating mechanism underlying the normal data.
  • Basic model assumes that outliers are rare observations, but there may be a large class of similar objects that are the outliers.
  • Outliers are also in the reference set and may falsify the results because mean and standard deviation are very sensitive to outliers.
